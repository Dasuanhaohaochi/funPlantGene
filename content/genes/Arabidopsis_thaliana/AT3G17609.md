## Germplasm Phenotype:
   - [cop1-1 hyh]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Indistinguishable from the respective cop1 mutants when grown in darkness.
   - [cop1-1 hyh]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;No suppression of the light-dependent cop1 phenotype where seedlings bleach and die when transferred from darkness to light. This is in contrast to the phenotype observed in the other two double mutants: cop1-4/hyh and cop1-6/hyh.
   - [cop1-4 hyh]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Indistinguishable from the respective cop1 mutants when grown in darkness.
   - [cop1-4 hyh]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Suppression in a substantial number of seedlings of the light-dependent cop1 phenotype where seedlings bleach and die when transferred from darkness to light.
   - [cop1-6 hyh]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Indistinguishable from the respective cop1 mutants when grown in darkness.
   - [cop1-6 hyh]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Suppression in a substantial number of seedlings of the light-dependent cop1 phenotype where seedlings bleach and die when transferred from darkness to light.
   - [hy5-KS50 hyh]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Blue-light-grown double-mutant seedlings have a longer hypocotyl than seedlings carrying the hyh or hy5 mutation alone.
   - [hy5-KS50 hyh]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Mutant produces significantly reduced levels of anthocyanins and is not responsive to cytokinins at all.
