## Germplasm Phenotype:
   - [nic2-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;A twofold decrease in  nicotinamidase activity was seen in dry seed extracts of the mutant. 
   - [nic2-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Freshly harvested nic2-1 seeds had a decreased  level of poly(ADP)ribosylation compared to Ws seeds. 
   - [nic2-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Mutant plants appeared  to grow and develop normally. Their seeds  phenotype have paler seed coat colour compared  to wild-type Ws but the embryo was the same colour as WT. 
   - [nic2-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Mutant seeds show  hypersensitivity to nicotinamide (inhibitor of seed germination). 
   - [nic2-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Significant 1.75-fold increase of NAD levels.
   - [nic2-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Treatment with  1 mM NADH promoted a small but significant increase in  germination of mutant seeds. 
   - [nic2-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Under moist-chilled (stratified) conditions, mutant seeds showed  a slightly retarded germination,  but there was no difference in the final percentage germination. However, in the absence of a moist  chilling pre-treatment (non-stratified), germination was delayed  and the germination potential of mutatn seeds was  significantly lower than that of Ws, indicating a  hyperdormant phenotype.
