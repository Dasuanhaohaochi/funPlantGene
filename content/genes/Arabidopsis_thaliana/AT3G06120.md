## Germplasm Phenotype:
   - [mute]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Complete absence of stomata.  Plants are small, pale and sterile.  Meristemoids undergo 3-6 rounds (vs. 1-3 rounds in wild type) of asymmetric division.  These divisions create a rosette pattern in the mute epidermis with an arrested, small triangular meristemoid at the center.
   - [mute]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Mutants express high levels of stomatal lineage cell markers TMM and ERL1.
