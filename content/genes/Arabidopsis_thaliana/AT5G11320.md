## Germplasm Phenotype:
   - [npy1-1;  yuc1;  yuc4-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;The triple mutant fails to produce cotyledons. True leaves develop, but show aberrant phyllotaxy. The mutant also fails to produce flowers on its pin-formed primary and lateral inflorescences. These phenotypes cannot be attributed to changes in the patterns of PIN1, PID, or MP transcription in the inflorescence.
   - [yuc1; yuc4-1; pid-101]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;This triple mutant fails to produce cotyledons, a defect that can first be observed at the early heart embryo developmental stage. True leaves develop, but show aberrant phyllotaxy. The mutant forms a normal hypocotyl and roots and produces a normal apical hook in the dark.
