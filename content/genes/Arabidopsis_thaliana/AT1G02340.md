   - [hfr1-101]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Fluence response curves in both R and FR light confirmed that rsf1 seedlings responded normally to R light and are less sensitive to all the tested fluences of FR light.    - [hfr1-101]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Hypocotyl elongation is less inhibited than in wild-type plants in both FR and blue light. Hypocotyl length in rsf1 seedlings was intermediate between the wild type and a photoreceptor null mutant under both light conditions.
   - [hfr1-101]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;In short days (SD), flowering time is unaffected. rsf1 plants were indistinguishable from wild type both in LD and SD using two criteria to measure flowering time (days until flowering and leaf number at flowering).
