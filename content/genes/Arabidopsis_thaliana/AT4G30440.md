## Germplasm Phenotype:
   - [CS69154]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Slightly smaller than wildtype; brittle leaves (leaves break easily); low GalA levels in the cell wall (reduced pectin content); susceptible to Botrytis cinerea.
   - [CS69155]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;No visible phenotype.
   - [gae1/gae6]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Double mutants have increased cellulose, Rha , Fuc, Ara, Xyl, Man, Glc, and Gal levels and  decreased GalA and homogalacturonan levels. Mutants are also more susceptible to specific Botrytis cinerea isolates.
