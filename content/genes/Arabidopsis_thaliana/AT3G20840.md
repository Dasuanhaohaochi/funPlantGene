   - [plt1-1]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;The mutation causes extra cells in the columella root cap and the QC.
   - [plt1-1/plt2-2]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Short roots due to premature stem cell differentiation. Increased telomere length likely resulting from fewer replicative stem cell divisions prior to differentiation.
   - [plt1-2]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;The mutation causes extra cells in the columella root cap and the QC.
   - [plt3-1/plt2-2/plt1-4]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Rootless.Embryonic root differentiates 3 days after germination. Adventitious roots arrest 6 days after germination.
   - [ptl1-4]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;sensitive to abscisic acid (ABA)
