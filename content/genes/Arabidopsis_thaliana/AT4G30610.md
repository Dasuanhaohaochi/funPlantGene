## Germplasm Phenotype:
   - [CS6127]()&nbsp;---&nbsp;Double mutant; multiple bri1-5 phenotype defects are suppressed by brs1-1D; doble mutants have primary inflorescence stems twice as long as those with bri1-5 alone and rosettes are wider because the leaves are bigger and not curled; brs1-1D suppresses both the shortened secondary inflorescence branch length phenotype  and the late flowering of bri1-5; other traits, which are unaffected in bri1-5 relative to wt plants such as flower number, coinflorescence number and branch number are not altered in doble mutant plants.
