## Functional Descriptions:
   - Transient expression of the green fluorescent protein fused **GmDR1** protein in soybean leaves showed that it is a plasma membrane protein.
   - In this study, can significantly enhance defence pathways among the **GmDR1**-overexpressed transgenic soybean lines.
   - Overexpression of **GmDR1** resulted in induced expression of the GmPR1-1 gene, a marker of the defence pathway regulated by SA.
   - Overexpression of **GmDR1** resulted in novel immunity against the spider mites.
   - Overexpression of **GmDR1** enhanced F. virguliforme resistance.
   - Overexpression of **GmDR1** resulted in enhanced immunity against soybean aphids.
